about me

I'm Nimish (/nɪmɪʃ/), a data scientist and a machine learning engineer obsessed with pushing the frontiers of what can be achieved with data.

My journey began with trying to understanding the world through physics. I then tried exploring the physics and chemistry of materials through computer simulations. Currently, I am trying to make sense of the much more than physical reality through the lense of data and tinker with it with AI/ML.

When I'm not coding at work, I am mostly updating on latest news in STEM or diving deep into the latest research papers around AI/ML.

interests & philosophy

I believe that technology is an important tool to improve life (not just mine, but those of the planet). But it isn't sufficient on its own. Lately, I've been contemplating on where, why, and how technology can be leveraged.

My hobbies are ephemeral, varies from reading sci-fi, to accurately recreating dishes that were historically consumed, to cycling 10s of kms if not 100s. My hobbies, however temporary, are always tangential to my full-time job.
