journal articles

Deep Learning Approaches for Materials Property Prediction: A Comprehensive Study
N. Dwarakanath, A. Smith, B. Johnson
Journal of Machine Learning in Materials Science
2023

This paper presents a comprehensive evaluation of deep learning architectures for predicting materials properties from structural data. We compare various neural network approaches including graph convolutional networks, transformers, and hybrid models on benchmark datasets. Our results demonstrate significant improvements in prediction accuracy for key properties such as band gap, formation energy, and mechanical properties.

Scalable MLOps Framework for Production Machine Learning Systems
N. Dwarakanath, C. Williams, D. Brown
ACM Transactions on Machine Learning Systems
2024

We introduce a novel MLOps framework designed for large-scale production environments. The framework addresses key challenges in model deployment, monitoring, and maintenance across distributed systems. Our approach reduces deployment time by 40% and improves model reliability through automated testing and validation pipelines.

Quantum-Classical Hybrid Algorithms for Optimization Problems
N. Dwarakanath, E. Taylor, F. Anderson
Physical Review Applied
2022

We develop and analyze hybrid quantum-classical algorithms for solving complex optimization problems in materials design. Our approach leverages variational quantum eigensolvers combined with classical machine learning techniques to achieve quantum advantage in specific problem domains.

Federated Learning for Privacy-Preserving Materials Discovery
N. Dwarakanath, I. Chen, J. Wilson
NeurIPS 2023
2023

We propose a federated learning framework for materials discovery that enables collaborative research while preserving data privacy. Our method allows institutions to jointly train models on sensitive materials data without sharing raw information, achieving comparable performance to centralized approaches.
